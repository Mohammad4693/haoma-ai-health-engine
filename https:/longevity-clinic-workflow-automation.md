# Longevity Clinic Workflow Automation

## Purpose

This document outlines the operational automation framework for preventive longevity clinics.

The system aims to reduce operational bottlenecks and improve patient journey efficiency using AI-assisted workflows.

---

# Patient Journey Flow

1. Lead Acquisition
2. Qualification
3. Initial Consultation
4. Lab Coordination
5. Biological Age Testing
6. AI Risk Analysis
7. Physician Review
8. Personalized Protocol
9. Follow-Up Monitoring
10. Retention & Subscription

---

# Lead Qualification Logic

## High Priority Leads
- high disposable income
- proactive health behavior
- executive lifestyle
- GCC residents
- recurring clinic visitors

## Medium Priority Leads
- wellness-focused individuals
- gym-oriented users
- preventative health interest

## Low Priority Leads
- price-sensitive users
- low engagement behavior
- incomplete intake forms
